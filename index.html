About Golimar Police Station

Golimar Thana is the frontline enforcement unit for its precinct. We combine rapid-field response, intelligence-driven investigations and community outreach to keep citizens safe.

Golimar Police Station operates 24/7 with specialized patrol units, tactical response teams and a dedicated investigation branch. With modern surveillance, mobile deployment and community liaisons, the station focuses on fast intervention, prevention tactics and victim support.

Our commitment: fair enforcement, timely justice and building trust with the public. We prioritize transparent investigations, accountability and continuous training for our officers to meet the evolving crime patterns.
