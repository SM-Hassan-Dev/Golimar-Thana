Who We Are

Golimar Police Station stands as a shield between peace and fear. Every officer here carries the pride of Pakistan by protecting the people of Golimar — day and night.

Humara maqsad crime ko jarr se khatam karna hai. Humari police aap ki himayat ke liye mojood hai — har waqt, har gali me.

Community policing hamari strategy ka sab se bara hissa hai. Jab awam sath deti hai — dushman kamzor pad jata hai. CCTV surveillance, field intelligence aur fast response ne humari success rate ko barhaya hai.
